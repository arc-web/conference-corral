The problem

What's broken without this

🤷
Walking in blind
Event organizers and facilitators have attendee lists but no context about who these people are, what they do, or how they connect to the event topic.
⏱️
Manual research takes hours
Digging through LinkedIn, company websites, and registration forms individually to build attendee context is repetitive work that scales poorly with room size.
❓
Missed connection opportunities
Without knowing attendee backgrounds beforehand, facilitators can't match people strategically, spot expertise gaps, or craft relevant opening conversations.

Under the hood

How it works

📥
Upload attendee list
Provide a spreadsheet with names, emails, and any registration form data you collected (job titles, company, experience level, etc.).
⚙️
Generate profiles
Conference Corral enriches each attendee with verified LinkedIn links, inferred role and company context, confidence scores, one-line conversation starters, and flags for edge cases.
📊
Get room map and dossiers
Receive individual profiles for each attendee plus an expertise map that groups people by skill tier so you can see your audience composition at a glance.
